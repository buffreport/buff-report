Submit a Story Tip

Got a scoop, a rumor worth chasing, or a story you think the CU Buffs community should know about? We want to hear it.

Please note: We read every tip that comes in, but we are a small operation and cannot follow up on or publish every submission. Anonymous tips are welcome — your name and contact info are always optional.

We will not add you to any mailing list. Only used if we need to reach out about your tip.

If there's a link, article, social post, or document backing up your tip, paste it here.

This will open your email client addressed to tips@buffreport.com.

Prefer to reach out another way? Email tips@buffreport.com directly or find us on X / Twitter @BuffReport.
