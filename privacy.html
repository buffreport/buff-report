Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

The Buff Report ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website buffreport.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information. The primary data we collect is:

  • Email addresses — collected when you voluntarily sign up for our newsletter. Submission of your email address is entirely optional.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymized data about site visits (pages viewed, general geographic region, device type) collected via analytics tools to help us improve the site. This data is not linked to any individual identity.

We do not require you to create an account to use The Buff Report. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

How We Use Your Information

Email addresses collected through newsletter signups are used solely to deliver The Buff Report newsletter. We use Beehiiv as our newsletter platform. Your email is transmitted to and stored by Beehiiv in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at beehiiv.com/privacy.

We do not use your email address for any purpose other than newsletter delivery. We will not add you to any list without your explicit consent.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. Your data is yours.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to improve your experience on The Buff Report. Specifically:

  • Preference cookies — used to remember your display preferences, such as dark mode settings, so they persist across visits.
  • Analytics cookies — used by our analytics provider to collect anonymized, aggregated data about site usage.

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features (such as saved preferences).

Third-Party Advertising

The Buff Report may display advertisements served by third-party ad partners, including but not limited to Tallow and Eyeserum. These partners may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing activity across sites. Their data collection and use is governed by their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

We do not share your personal information (including your email address) with advertisers.

Newsletter Unsubscribe

You may unsubscribe from The Buff Report newsletter at any time. Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Clicking that link will immediately remove your email from our mailing list. You can also request removal by contacting us directly at admin@buffreport.com.

Data Retention

We retain newsletter subscriber email addresses for as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribing, your email is removed from our active list. Beehiiv's data retention policies may also apply; please refer to their privacy policy for details.

Links to External Sites

The Buff Report is a news aggregator. The majority of content links off-site to third-party publications and sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
