Our Sources

The Buff Report aggregates headlines and links from dozens of trusted publications. We do not produce original reporting — we surface the best coverage from around the web and link directly back to the sources doing the work.

Content ownership: All articles, reporting, photography, and original analysis belong to the respective publications and journalists who created them. The Buff Report displays only headlines and brief summaries, and every link takes you to the original source. If you are a publisher with questions about how your content is displayed, please contact us.
